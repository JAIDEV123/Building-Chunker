Building Chunker

Chunking is an analysis of a sentence which identifies the constituents (noun groups, verbs, verb groups, etc.) which are correlated. These are non-overlapping regions of text.
The objective of the experiment is to know the importance of selecting proper features for training a model and size of training corpus in learning how to do cunking.
